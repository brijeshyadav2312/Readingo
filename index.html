This Website For All The Reading Addicts

I absolutely love to read but I don’t very often because it is a bad addiction for me. When I read a book, I lose focus on everything else. I will sit in bed and read until I have finished either the whole book or else whole series if there is more. This is hard because I won’t eat, won’t sleep, don’t care about the real world, won’t talk to anyone and if I do stop reading I can’t focus on reality and my mind is only on the book. Because of all of this I will hardly read because it’s too hard for me to stop and I know i can’t let myself read series or else I will be “out of commission” until I’ve read them all. Am I the only one like this? It’s so hard because I love to read but it effects my life so much since I can’t seem to be able to balance reading and real life!
